# Core Risk & Governance Principles
* **ISO 31000** sets risk‑management principles, framework, and process [ISO31000].
* **COSO ERM 2017** links risk to strategy and performance [COSO-ERM].
* **NIST SP 800‑30** provides detailed risk‑assessment steps [NIST80030].
* **FAIR** enables quantitative risk in financial terms [FAIR].
